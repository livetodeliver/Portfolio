Hi, I’m Stacy. I’m a web developer who loves clean code and creative problem-solving.

A little about me.

Before the days of Facebook, HTML5, and even MySpace, my dad’s passion for computers became contagious and as a preteen I began creating my own websites. I used it as a way to connect with the world far beyond my bedroom walls. Now, many years later, I have resparked that adolescent fire I had for web development and have found myself drawn back to coding as a place where my passion for learning something new is constantly fed.

When not tapping away at computer keys I can be found doing a myriad of activities such as “Bookstagramming,” learning pointed pen calligraphy, artisan soapmaking, vegan cooking, snuggling with my furry family (2 dogs, 3 cats), and maybe even doing something physical like a long bike ride, or a rowing class.
