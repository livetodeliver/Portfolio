Hi, I’m Stacy. I’m a Philly based web developer with a passion for dynamic UX design, and using creative approaches to solving tough problems.

A little bit about me.

Before the days of Facebook, HTML5, and even MySpace, my dad’s passion for computers became contagious and as a pre-teen I began creating my own websites. I used it as a way to connect with the world far beyond my bedroom walls. Now, many years later, I have resparked that adolescent fire I had for web development and have found myself drawn back to coding as a place where my passion for learning something new is constantly fed.

I'm searching for a thriving workplace where I can bring my positive energy, knowledge and skills to a strong and diverse team. I would love to be in a environment that encourages authenticity, learning, and growth.

When not tapping away at computer keys I can be found doing a myriad of activities such as “Bookstagramming,” learning pointed pen calligraphy, artisan soapmaking, vegan cooking, snuggling with my furry family (2 dogs, 3 cats), and maybe even doing something physical like a long bike ride, or a rowing class.

I am also in love with Gritty, The Philadelphia Flyers mascot, and you probably should be, too.

Vegan-Friendly PHL App

This app utilizes the VegGuide.org Rest API, Google Maps API, and Google Places API to find vegan and vegan-friendly restaurant locations within the Philadelphia area. Users have the ability to select certain search paramaters to filter results, and then are given a map that can display the location for a selected result entry. Users may choose to search by distance based on a given address, by selected neighbhorhood from a dropdown menu, or simply just do a blanket search for any vegan-friendly restaurants in Philadelphia that meet their other selected criteria.
